Shake

Earthquake Warning Notifier for Windows, Mac and Linux.


Shake is an Earthquake Early Warning program for Windows, Linux and OS X.
With in-built desktop notifications and an alerting pop-up window with the Earthquake's preliminary info, Shake is an easy-to-use program that puts your safety first.
